Guidelines

  • Respect:Treat others with kindness and respect.
  • Confidentiality: Respect the privacy of your peers.
  • Participation: Engage actively in conversations and activities.
  • Support: Offer help when you can.

  • Inclusivity: Make everyone feel welcome.

  • Boundaries: Recognize personal boundaries.
